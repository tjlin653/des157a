An Evil Dragon has come to destroy your village!
But only the Holy Sword can slay the dragon.

A merchant will sell the sword for 30 Magic Gems.
And each turn, they will deal 2 cards.

You must then choose between an Effect Card
(on the left) and a Gem Card (on the right).

The effect card is high-risk, high-reward opportunity,
while the gem card will provide a gem value.

Gather enough gems by the 10th Turn to win!
