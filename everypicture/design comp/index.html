For my Every Picture Project, I went to the Raptor Center here at UCD to find a few good groups of photographs that I could use for stop motion. I have a few groups of photos from this trip of raptors feeding on small rodents. Through these photos, I want to elicit a feeling of mystery and eeriness. I also want these pictures to connect to my overall theme of the illusion of control.

When experimenting with design ideas for my project, I did not yet know what I wanted the end product to look like. What I have in mind now, however, is a webpage with at least three rows of photos that slowly drift by horizontally.
